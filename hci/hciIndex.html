Overall our group was very communicative throughout the entire process. The original idea of "a day in the life of trunp" was a result of one of my original sketches. And it was interesting to see how that idea evolved through our itteration of sketches. For example, eventually we decided to drop the "morning" to "evening" to "night" aspects of the button and instead decided to opt for simple schenes that showcased what trump might be up to at any given moments. Eventually at the end of the day we decided to make all of these day in the life scenes interactive. So then we could combine some of the ideas from our original sketches. (Tweets, wall building etc) with our current main idea.

For the actual project itself I helped to find images to use for our different scenes. We also went through several iterations of buttons before we got to the "maga hat" button. Before that the buttons that I found were a trump face and a make america great again button.

Coding wise I helped with animations such as the trip adviser animation, the wll animation. the miss america text animation and a few other animations that eventually we decided to cut out of our final project like a closed for bankruptcy animation and a tipping wine glass animation.
